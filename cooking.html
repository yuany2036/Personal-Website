Taiwanese Beef Noodle

Click on the image above to get a more professional recipe

Ingredients Needed:

  • Beef
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Spring onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Ginger
  • Chili
  • Doubanjiang
  • Soy sauce
  • Dark soy sauce
  • Rice wine

Steps:

  1. Add oil to pot, and stir fry the onions until softened.
  2. Add in the garlic, spring onions, ginger, chili and continue to stir fry.
  3. Add the Doubanjiang to the pot, mix well, and then add in the tomatoes.
  4. Pour in the dark soy sauce, soy sauce and rice wine, let the veggies and sauce simmer for a few minutes before adding the beef to the pot.
  5. Cook some water to boiling point, add to pot until it covers all contents within.
  6. Leave on simmer for an hour, and then serve with noodles.
  7. Enjoy!

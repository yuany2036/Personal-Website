What is the most complex board game I own?

The most complex board game that I own is called Twilight Imperium. It is a grand strategy game that can be played by upwards of 6 players, and the main goal is to become the new emperor of the galaxy.

The game has a great number of components to it, and can take upwards of 10 hours to finish, so it is a game you really want to set aside a day for.
